Level 5: Fractions 🖩 Use Calc
Fraction of an Amount
Forward: To find $\frac{2}{3}$ of 60, calculate $60 \div 3 \times 2$.
Reverse: If 10 is $\frac{1}{4}$ of a number, calculate $10 \times 4$ to find the total.

Question A (Forward): Calculate $\frac{3}{5}$ of 150.

Solution:
Divide by bottom, multiply by top.
150 ÷ 5 × 3 =
Result: 90

Question B (Reverse): 24 is $\frac{2}{3}$ of a mystery number. What is the mystery number?

Solution (Reverse):
If 2 parts = 24, then 1 part = 12 ($24 \div 2$).
The whole is 3 parts, so $12 \times 3 = 36$.
24 ÷ 2 × 3 =
Result: 36
Level 6: Ratios 🖩 Use Calc
Sharing in a Ratio
To share an amount in ratio 2:3:
1. Add parts: $2+3=5$
2. Divide total by parts: $\text{Total} \div 5 = \text{1 share}$
3. Multiply: $\times 2$ and $\times 3$.

Question: Share $500 in the ratio 2:3.

What is the larger share?

Solution:
1. Total parts: $2 + 3 = 5$.
2. One share: $500 \div 5 = 100$.
3. Larger share (3 parts): $100 \times 3 = 300$.
500 ÷ 5 × 3 =
Result: $300
Level 7: Rates 🖩 Use Calc
The Unitary Method
To solve rate problems, find the value of ONE unit first.

Question: 4 kg of apples cost $14.00. How much do 7 kg cost?

Solution:
1. Find cost of 1 kg: $14 \div 4 = 3.5$.
2. Find cost of 7 kg: $3.5 \times 7 = 24.5$.
14 ÷ 4 × 7 =
Result: $24.50

HW Q4 (Proportional Rate)
Proportion: A walking team travels 35km in 3 days.

At the same rate, how far would they travel in 2 days? (Round to 1 d.p)

Solution:
First find 1 day: $35 \div 3 = 11.666...$
Then multiply by 2: $11.666 \times 2 = 23.333...$
Result: 23.3 km

HW Q6 (Ratio Context)
Mixing Drinks: You mix syrup and water in the ratio 1:4.

You make 500mL of drink in total. How much syrup did you use?

Solution:
1. Total parts: $1 + 4 = 5$.
2. One part (Syrup): $500 \div 5 = 100$.
Result: 100 mL
HW Q7 (Reverse Fraction)
Spending Money: You spent $\frac{3}{5}$ of your money on a gift. The gift cost $30.

How much money did you have originally?

Solution (Reverse):
If 3 parts = $30, then 1 part = $10 ($30 \div 3$).
Total is 5 parts, so $10 \times 5 = 50$.
Result: $50
